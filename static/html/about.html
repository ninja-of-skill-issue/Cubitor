What is Speedcubing?

Speedcubing is the sport of solving a variety of combination puzzles, most notably the 3x3x3 Rubik's Cube, as quickly as possible. For most puzzles, solving involves performing a series of sequences of moves, called algorithms.

The sport has grown into a global phenomenon with thousands of competitions held worldwide by the World Cube Association (WCA). Cubitor was built to provide cubers of all levels with the tools they need to master their craft, from advanced timers to algorithm trainers.
