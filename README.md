# 💠 Cubitor

### The Ultimate Speedcubing Dashboard & Learning Suite
**Cubitor** is a professional-grade, web-based platform designed for speedcubers of all levels. Whether you're tracking your latest solves, learning new algorithm sets, or competing in high-stakes minigames, Cubitor provides the tools you need to break your personal bests.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### ⏱️ Professional Timer
- **High-Precision Timing:** Real-time feedback with millisecond accuracy.
- **WCA Regulation Support:** Built-in inspection timers, +2, and DNF penalty management.
- **Dynamic Scrambles:** Powered by `Cubing.js`, providing official-spec scrambles for all WCA events (3x3, 4x4, OH, Megaminx, and more).

### 📊 Advanced Analytics
- **Live Statistics:** Track your Ao5, Ao12, Ao50, and Ao100 in real-time.
- **Interactive Charts:** Visualize your progress over time with dynamic line charts and distribution histograms.
- **Session Management:** Organize your solves into folders and events for structured training.

### 🧠 Learning Dashboard
- **Algorithm Library:** Comprehensive lists for PLL, OLL, F2L, and more.
- **Custom Sets:** Create and manage your own algorithm sets (e.g., Winter Variation, CLL).
- **Interactive Visualizers:** 3D and Top-down cube views to help you memorize cases faster.

### 🎮 Cubing Minigames
- **Time Attack:** Solve as many as you can within a 2-minute window.
- **Elimination:** Stay consistent—don't be the slowest solve in the set!
- **Scramble Challenge:** Match the visual scramble sequence under pressure.

---

## 🎨 Personalization
- **Theme Engine:** Choose from Sleek Dark, Pure White, Deep Blue, Forest Green, and more.
- **Typography:** Multiple font options (Elegant, Tech, Mono, and Consolas) to fit your aesthetic.
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for both desktop setups and mobile practice.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Core:** HTML5, CSS3 (Vanilla), JavaScript (ES6+)
- **Visualization:** [Cubing.js](https://cubing.js.org/) for high-fidelity 3D rendering and scrambles.
- **Data:** Chart.js for beautiful performance analytics.
- **Storage:** LocalStorage persistence for seamless offline-first usage.
